About Me

I am a passionate researcher and mechatronics engineer with expertise in artificial intelligence, machine learning, and computer vision.

My research focuses on diffusion models, image editing & inpainting, vision transformers, medical image generation, and continual learning. I have experience working with international research teams across Australia, Japan, and Bangladesh, where I have contributed to a range of projects in the fields of diffusion models, image editing, medical image analysis, and continual learning. These collaborations have strengthened my ability to conduct interdisciplinary research, adapt to diverse academic environments, and contribute meaningfully to ongoing projects in a globally connected research setting.
